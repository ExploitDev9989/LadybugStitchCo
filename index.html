A Little About Us 🐞

Ladybug Stitch Co. was born from love, memory, and a passion for creating. As a child, I spent hours in my Granny Jean’s craft room, learning to sew and bring ideas to life. She was the one who sparked my love for creating and the nickname I’ll always cherish: her little ladybug. Today, every piece I create is inspired by those moments. Each stitch is a small way of honoring her legacy through thoughtful, handcrafted designs made with care.

Whether it's a wedding gift, a nursery piece, a pet portrait, or a personalized keepsake. If you can dream it, we can stitch it!
